About the Company

Ampd Energy is an award-winning energy storage company ensuring Asian businesses and brands see the light and stay switched on. Our revolutionary Ampd Silo means power to empower. Co-founded by electric visionaries Brandon Ng and Luca Valente and based in Hong Kong, Ampd designs, engineers and makes state-of-the-art, grid-connected energy storage systems.

Our Mission

To provide clean, affordable and most importantly stable electricity for the three billion people and millions of enterprises that lack access to reliable power

Our Origins

Ampd Energy was founded in 2014 by Brandon Ng and Luca Valente, a pair of visionaries who are switched on by power. This is their second company founded together; the first developed high performance electric motorcycles. The lightbulb moment for Ampd Energy was a blackout, during which they realised their motorcycle EV batteries could be scaled to power buildings. From darkness came light, and compact, uninterrupted back up power supply for businesses and brands all over Asia, and beyond.

Our Founders

Brandon Ng

Chief Executive Officer

Brandon holds a Master's degree in Chemical Engineering from Imperial College London, where he graduated with First-Class Honours and was awarded the Governors' Prize in Chemical Engineering and the City & Guilds Gold Medal for Excellence. Winner of the 2017 Forbes Asia 30 Under 30, his background includes founding two start-ups, advising renewable energy investment and development projects at the Environmental Justice Foundation and working as a proprietary equity derivatives trader at Barclays Capital in London.

Luca Valente

Chief Operating Officer

Luca holds a Master's in Mechanical Engineering from the Pontiff Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro and an MBA from the Cheung Kong Graduate School of Business. Before Ampd Energy, Luca worked for Volkswagen and Embraer. Luca moved to China in 2005, where he led the engineering team at Harbin Embraer Aircraft Industry, the joint venture between Embraer and Aviation Industry Corporation of China.
