Enterprise ready

Ampd Silo is designed for commercial use. With a parallel (N+X) scaling there is no need to over-size your UPS system, just add additional Ampd Silo when needed. True flexibility. Peerless efficiency. Remote performance management allows us to keep Ampd Silo constantly updated.

We manage it for you – but you can track real-time performance from anywhere in the world. And with MODBUS integration, Ampd Silo integrates seamlessly with your building management software.

Safe and reliable

Ampd Silo uses the most advanced automotive grade Li-ion batteries to assure the UPS can deliver maximum power when needed. Safety is our priority – seven autonomous fail safe features assure Ampd Silo is never compromised. This is in line with aerospace safety principles, and no less than your business deserves. Ampd Silo is the only UPS provider in Asia to secure UL9540 certification, backed up by adherence to CE, IEC and UN standards.

By using multiple Silos, instead of one large battery, you are afforded the protection of (N+1), removing a single failure point. This is known as Redundant Power Protection. You might prefer to think of it as the ultimate peace of mind.

Saves space

Ampd Silo is designed to fit into existing power backup space configurations. Two Silos fit into a 19″ 42U rack space, removing any need for structural changes.

Switch to Ampd Silo … and switch on savings of up to 80 per cent of your office or building space. Free up space for more valuable uses, or take the opportunity to save on real estate costs.

Maintenance free

Apart from being noisy and polluting, diesel generators are notoriously bad for maintenance, putting your business at risk of losing power when faced with a blackout. Ampd Silo self-maintains, using software updates, which we manage remotely for you, removing all hassle and headache.

Lead acid batteries need to be changed every 3 years, making them impractical to use in remote locations and difficult to replace. Install Ampd Silo, and savour instant, maintenance-free peace of mind for decades. Plug it in. Switch in on. And get on with the job.
